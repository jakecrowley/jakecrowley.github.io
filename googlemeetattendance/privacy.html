Google Meet Attendance Extension Privacy Policy

Updated April 13, 2020

The Google Meet Attendance Extension was created by Jake Crowley. The extension does not save any data outside of your computer or send it anywhere and was made specifically with teachers in mind to provide a tool to record attendance with little effort.

The source code is issued with a MIT license and hosted publicly on GitHub. Pull requests for the extension will be considered. Any parties with concerns may view the source code.
